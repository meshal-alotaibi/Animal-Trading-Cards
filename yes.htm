Horse

Horses have lived on Earth for more than 50 million years, according the American Museum of Natural History.

  • Name: Horse
  • Average Height: 1.4-1.8m
  • Average weight: 380-1000kg
  • Average lifespan: 25-30 years

Horses have been domesticated for at least 5000 years. They have been used by humans in many different ways for travel, work, food, and pleasure. Cavalry horses were used in war until the middle 20th century.
